# Tech Salary Benchmarks Across MENA (2026)

As the MENA tech ecosystem matures, salary bands are polarizing based on location, funding stage, and role specialization.

## 2026 Projections (Annual, USD)

- **UAE/KSA Local:** Senior Software Engineer ($85k - $130k), Product Manager ($90k - $140k).
- **Jordan/Egypt (Remote to GCC):** Senior Software Engineer ($45k - $75k).
- **Global Remote to MENA:** Highly specialized roles, particularly in AI/ML and Data Engineering, command $100k+.

## The Takeaway

Significant arbitrage opportunities exist. Companies in the UAE and KSA can secure top-decile talent in Jordan and Egypt at a 40% discount to local hiring, while simultaneously offering those developers a 50% premium over their local market rates. It's a win-win enabled by remote-first platforms.
