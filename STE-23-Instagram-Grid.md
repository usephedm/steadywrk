# STEADYWRK (@swrk.jo) - Instagram Launch Strategy & 9-Post Grid

## Strategy Overview
**Brand Voice:** Ambitious, direct, no fluff.
**Objective:** Launch the STEADYWRK Instagram account with a powerful 9-post grid that immediately establishes authority, explains the value proposition (The AI BPO Revolution in Jordan), attracts top 1% talent, and highlights the US market connection.

*Instagram feeds are read top-to-bottom, but built bottom-to-top. Post 1 is the first post published (bottom right).*
