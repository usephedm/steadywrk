# STE-43: PDPL Compliance (Jordanian Data Privacy Law)

## 1. Top Jordanian Law Firms Specializing in Tech & Data Privacy

The enforcement of the **Jordan Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL) No. 24 of 2023** ended its one-year grace period on March 17, 2025. Non-compliance could result in substantial fines or license revocation. For specialized guidance regarding STEADYWRK's compliance, the following top-tier and boutique firms are recommended:

*   **Jaradat Lawyers (Jaradat & Associates):** Highly specialized in the 2023 PDPL, offering comprehensive data protection audits, policy drafting, and Data Protection Officer (DPO) services. Excellent choice for dedicated compliance programs.
*   **Ali Sharif Zu’bi Advocates & Legal Consultants:** A "Top Tier" firm with extensive experience in handling complex regulatory issues, high-stakes compliance, and technology transactions. Best for large-scale operations.
*   **Al Tamimi & Company:** A leading regional firm with a robust TMT (Technology, Media, and Telecommunications) practice in Amman. Strong expertise in cross-border data transfers and cloud computing.
*   **Dentons (in association with Safwan Moubaydeen Law Firm):** Provides global-standard expertise locally. Ideal for ensuring alignment between local Jordanian law and international data privacy frameworks like the GDPR.
*   **Nsair & Partners:** A boutique firm with a heavy focus on the digital economy. They offer tailored DPO services and have experience helping tech startups navigate data regulations in the MENA region.

**Recommendation for STEADYWRK:** Consider engaging **Jaradat Lawyers** for focused, actionable PDPL compliance audits and DPO support, or **Al Tamimi & Company** if dealing with extensive cross-border data processing.

We are pleased to formally appoint you as the **Data Protection Officer (DPO)** for **STEADYWRK** (the "Company"), effective as of [Start Date].

This appointment is made pursuant to the requirements of the **Jordanian Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL) No. 24 of 2023**, its executive regulations, and the relevant guidelines issued by the Ministry of Digital Economy and Entrepreneurship (MoDEE) and the Personal Data Protection Unit.

### 1. Position and Status
You will report directly to the [Board of Directors / CEO / Highest Management Level] and act independently in the execution of your duties. The Company commits to providing you with the necessary resources, access to personal data processing operations, and ongoing training to maintain your expert knowledge.

### 2. Core Responsibilities
As the DPO, your primary duties will include, but are not limited to:
*   **Monitoring Compliance:** Ensuring the Company’s ongoing compliance with the provisions of the PDPL No. 24 of 2023 and the directives of the Personal Data Protection Council.
*   **Advisory:** Providing expert advice on data protection impact assessments, privacy-by-design implementations, and the lawful processing of sensitive personal data.
*   **Liaison:** Acting as the primary point of contact between the Company, the data subjects, and the Personal Data Protection Unit at MoDEE.
*   **Breach Management:** Overseeing the notification processes in the event of a personal data breach, ensuring adherence to the statutory timelines (24 hours to data subjects and 72 hours to the Unit for high-risk breaches).
*   **Registry Management:** Ensuring the Company is correctly registered and maintained within the official national registry of data controllers and processors.

### 3. Accreditation and Registration
Where required by the risk profile of our data processing activities, the Company will support you in fulfilling the DPO Accreditation Standards established by MoDEE. We will also formally notify the Personal Data Protection Directorate of your appointment, including your name and contact details, to be entered into the official registry.

### 4. Acceptance
Please signify your acceptance of this appointment and the responsibilities outlined herein by signing and returning a copy of this letter.

We look forward to working closely with you to ensure the highest standards of data privacy and security at STEADYWRK.
