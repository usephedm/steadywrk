# Why Jordan's Tech Talent Gap is an Opportunity

Jordan produces over 5,000 ICT graduates annually, yet local enterprises face a perceived "seniority gap." Rather than a weakness, this presents a massive opportunity for strategic nearshoring.

## The Data

- **Brain Drain:** 60% of senior developers in Jordan migrate to the GCC or remote EU/US roles within three years of reaching seniority.
- **Demand Mismatch:** Local demand for cloud-native engineers outstrips supply by 3:1.

## The Strategic Opportunity

Companies leveraging offshore/nearshore models can tap into Jordan's motivated mid-level talent, upskilling them rapidly. Jordan's timezone (GMT+3) perfectly overlaps with major EU hubs and GCC markets, enabling synchronous collaboration at a fraction of local hiring costs.
