## The Most In-Demand AI Roles

The era of the "generalist software engineer" is fading. The market demands deep specialization in AI-centric domains.

### 1. AI Systems Engineer
This is the builder. AI Systems Engineers do not just call APIs; they architect the infrastructure that supports large language models (LLMs) and computer vision systems. They optimize inference, manage GPU compute clusters, and build the scaffolding that makes AI models deployable at scale.
*   **Core Stack:** Python, Rust, CUDA, Kubernetes, Ray, vLLM.
*   **The Mandate:** You must understand how to squeeze every ounce of performance out of hardware. Cost-efficient inference is the name of the game.

### 2. Data Infrastructure Architect
AI models are only as good as the data they are trained on. Data Infrastructure Architects design and maintain the pipelines that ingest, clean, and vectorize massive datasets.
*   **Core Stack:** Apache Kafka, Snowflake, Databricks, dbt, Apache Airflow.
*   **The Mandate:** Build resilient systems that can handle petabytes of unstructured data without breaking. Data quality and pipeline latency are your primary KPIs.

### 3. Applied AI Researcher / Fine-Tuning Specialist
While foundational models (like GPT-5, Claude 4) are built by tech giants, the real value lies in fine-tuning these models for specific enterprise use cases.
*   **Core Stack:** PyTorch, Hugging Face, LoRA, QLoRA, DeepSpeed.
*   **The Mandate:** Take an open-source or proprietary model and adapt it to perform flawlessly on domain-specific tasks (legal, medical, financial) using constrained compute resources.

### 4. AI Product Manager
The bridge between engineering and business value. AI Product Managers understand the capabilities and limitations of current AI models and translate them into features that drive revenue.
*   **Core Skills:** Deep understanding of AI limitations, rapid prototyping, strong analytical skills, market sizing.
*   **The Mandate:** Prevent engineering from building "cool science projects" and force focus on features that solve immediate, high-value customer problems.
