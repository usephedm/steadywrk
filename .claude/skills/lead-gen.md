# Skill: Lead Gen
Trigger: leads, prospects, outreach, find clients, pipeline, cold email
## Context
SteadyWrk targets businesses that outsource field service dispatch — HVAC, plumbing, electrical, appliance repair, IT field services. US-based, overflow or third-party dispatch.
## Steps
1. Research vertical with web search (3+ queries)
2. Identify decision-makers: Ops Manager, Dispatch Manager, Owner
3. Build profile: company, size, services, current dispatch method
4. Score 1-10: outsourcing likelihood, size, volume
5. Draft outreach — short, benefit-first, zero fluff
6. Save to docs/content/leads/
## Outreach Rules
- Subject: 8 words max
- First line: reference something specific about their business
- Value prop: one sentence, quantified
- CTA: one ask
- Total: under 100 words
- Never: "hope this finds you well", "touching base", "circle back"
