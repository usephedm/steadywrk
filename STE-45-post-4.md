# How AI Resume Screening Works (And Why It's Fairer)

AI resume screening has evolved far beyond basic keyword matching; it now relies on deep semantic analysis to evaluate candidates.

## The Mechanics

- **Skill Extraction:** Natural Language Processing (NLP) extracts technical skills and normalizes them (e.g., recognizing "React.js" and "ReactJS" as identical).
- **Contextual Scoring:** The AI evaluates the depth of experience by analyzing the surrounding context. For instance, "Led a team using React" scores significantly higher than "Familiar with React".

## The Fairness Factor

Human recruiters spend an average of 7 seconds per resume and are often influenced by unconscious biases related to names, universities, or formatting. AI, conversely, evaluates 100% of the text based on objective, pre-defined criteria aligned directly with the job description, ensuring a merit-based initial screening process.
