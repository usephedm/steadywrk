# AI is Reshaping MENA Recruitment in 2026

The MENA region is experiencing a 45% year-over-year increase in AI adoption within HR operations. By 2026, 70% of enterprise tech companies in Dubai, Riyadh, and Amman will utilize AI-driven Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS).

## Key Drivers

- **Time-to-Hire Reduction:** AI screening reduces the average time-to-hire from 42 days to 14 days, a critical metric in the fast-paced tech sector.
- **Bias Mitigation:** Blind screening algorithms strip demographic data, focusing purely on skill validation and leveling the playing field.
- **Predictive Attrition:** AI models now predict candidate tenure with 82% accuracy, significantly reducing turnover costs.
